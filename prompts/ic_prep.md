# IC Preparation — Agent Playbook

## Objective

Prepare investment committee materials that enable partners to make an informed go/no-go decision within a 30-minute discussion.

## When This Runs

After DD is complete and the deal lead recommends proceeding. Inputs are the DD report, sourcing brief, financial model, and any partner notes.

## Memo Structure

The IC memo follows this fixed structure. Do not deviate from the order:

1. **Executive Summary** (half page max)
   - What the company does, in one sentence.
   - Why we should invest, in two sentences.
   - Deal terms: round size, valuation, the fund's proposed check, ownership target.
   - Key risk in one sentence.

2. **Company Overview** (1 page)
   - Founding story and team backgrounds.
   - Product description with current TRL.
   - Traction metrics: revenue, customers, pipeline, partnerships.
   - Key milestones achieved and next 12-month milestones.

3. **Market Analysis** (1 page)
   - TAM/SAM/SOM with methodology.
   - Competitive landscape map (table format: competitor, funding, stage, differentiation).
   - Market timing argument: why this company, why now.

4. **Financial Analysis** (1 page)
   - Historical financials (if any): revenue, burn, runway.
   - Forward projections: 3-year revenue model with assumptions stated explicitly.
   - Unit economics: current and projected at scale.
   - Use of proceeds breakdown.

5. **Deal Terms** (half page)
   - Instrument (SAFE, equity, convertible note).
   - Valuation (pre/post).
   - Key terms: pro-rata rights, board seat, information rights, anti-dilution.
   - Co-investors and their check sizes.

6. **Risk Assessment** (half page)
   - Top 3 risks with traffic-light ratings from DD.
   - Mitigants for each risk.
   - Scenario analysis: what happens if key assumption fails.

7. **Portfolio Context** (half page)
   - Overlap with existing portfolio companies. Direct conflict check.
   - Complementary opportunities: could this company partner with or sell to portfolio companies?
   - Sector concentration: does this deal push us beyond target allocation for any sector?

8. **Recommendation** (3 sentences max)
   - Clear invest / pass / conditional invest with stated conditions.

## Evidence Standards

- Every factual claim must cite its source (data room document, customer interview, public filing, analyst report).
- No unsupported assertions. "The market is growing" must become "The European industrial robotics market grew 12% CAGR 2022-2025 (IFR World Robotics 2025)."
- Projections must state their assumptions. "Revenue reaches EUR 5M by 2028 assuming 15% monthly growth from current EUR 40K MRR" not "Revenue will reach EUR 5M."
- If data is unavailable, say so explicitly. "Customer retention data not provided; requested from founder" is acceptable. Guessing is not.

## Presentation Standards

- Total memo length: 5-6 pages. Not longer.
- Use tables for competitive landscape and financial data. Prose for narrative sections.
- Bold key numbers and conclusions so partners can skim.
- Include a one-slide visual summary (company positioning map or key metrics dashboard) as an appendix.

## Pre-IC Checklist

Before submitting to IC, verify:
- [ ] All DD sections rated (Green/Amber/Red)
- [ ] Cap table modeled with the fund's entry
- [ ] At least 2 back-channel references completed
- [ ] Legal review of term sheet initiated
- [ ] Portfolio overlap check completed
- [ ] Financial model stress-tested against downside scenario
