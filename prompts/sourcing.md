# Deal Sourcing — Agent Playbook

## Objective

Scan for early-stage investment opportunities that match the fund's thesis and surface the strongest candidates as structured one-page briefs.

## Thesis Criteria

- **Geography:** Europe (EU + UK + Switzerland + Nordics). Prioritize companies with R&D in Europe even if incorporated elsewhere.
- **Stage:** Seed to Series A. Check size: target rounds of EUR 1-5M.
- **Model:** B2B. Enterprise or infrastructure sales. No consumer, no marketplace-only models.
- **Team:** At least one technical co-founder with deep domain expertise (PhD, industry R&D, prior deeptech startup). Assess founder-market fit explicitly.
- **Technology:** Proprietary or defensible. Must have IP, trade secrets, or a meaningful technical moat — not just a software wrapper.

## Sectors of Interest

- Advanced materials (composites, metamaterials, novel polymers)
- Quantum computing (hardware, error correction, quantum-safe cryptography)
- Synthetic biology (industrial biotech, biomanufacturing, enzyme engineering)
- Robotics (industrial automation, autonomous systems, manipulation)
- Photonics (LiDAR, optical computing, sensing)
- Adjacent deeptech that fits the profile (e.g., novel semiconductor architectures, space tech with B2B applications)

## Screening Heuristics

1. **Team:** Verify founder backgrounds via LinkedIn and publication records. Look for 5+ years domain experience or notable academic output.
2. **IP / Moat:** Check patent filings, published research, and technical blog posts. Is the core technology reproducible by a well-funded competitor in 18 months?
3. **Market timing:** Why now? Identify the enabling trend (regulatory shift, cost curve crossing, platform maturity).
4. **Unit economics potential:** Even at pre-revenue, sketch a plausible path to positive gross margins above 60%.
5. **Signal strength:** Warm referral from trusted source > conference discovery > cold inbound. Weight accordingly.

## Sources

- **Dealroom:** Filter by European deeptech, recent funding events, seed/Series A.
- **Crunchbase:** Cross-reference for funding history, investor overlap, and founder background.
- **Inbound referrals:** Tag by referral source and track conversion rate per source over time.
- **Conference attendee lists:** Focus on sector-specific events (Photonics West, SynBioBeta, European Robotics Forum, QCi).
- **Academic spinouts:** Monitor tech transfer offices at ETH, EPFL, TU Delft, Imperial, Cambridge, Max Planck institutes.
- **Existing portfolio founders:** Ask portfolio companies who they find impressive in adjacent spaces.

## Output Format

One-page company brief per candidate:

- **Company name, location, founding year, team size**
- **One-line description** (what they do in plain language)
- **Thesis fit score** (Strong / Moderate / Weak) with 2-3 sentence justification
- **Founding team** (names, backgrounds, domain relevance)
- **Technology summary** (what it is, TRL level, defensibility assessment)
- **Market context** (TAM estimate, key competitors, why-now trigger)
- **Funding status** (last round, current raise, known investors)
- **Recommended next step** (pass, watch, request intro, schedule call)
